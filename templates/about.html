About

dialassist is phone service that wants to help you stay in touch with those you care about. Use it to schedule weekly, bi-weekly or monthly phone calls.

dialassist was formed at Start-up Weekend Boston October, 2011 by Nate Aune, Teddy Wing, John Coogan, SuiLin Yap, Nick Pigott and Steve Hershman.
